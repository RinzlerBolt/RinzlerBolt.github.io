My Awards

BeTamworth Award

On behalf of Coliseum Cards, In 2018 I was proudly awarded the BeTamworth Award along with the team at Coliseum Cards. The award was presented by the then Mayor Richard Kingston, the award given was for our work in the local commmunity for adults and children with additional needs, also for creating a positive impact on the town of Tamworth when we host major tournamnets, bringing players for all over the country to use the local shops and cafes. My role was to teach children and adults how to play the trading card games. With the work of Sharon and Mark Lawson who are the owners, we made a safe environment for our players and visitors.

About Me

Hi, I'm Jake Lawson, a passionate and self-motivated Game Developer. I dedicate my time to mastering both new and existing programming languages, with a particular focus on C++ and C#. However, I'm always eager to explore a wide range of languages, whether they're older or cutting-edge. I enjoy diving into software, breaking down code to understand its intricacies, and applying that knowledge to create engaging software and games. My research interests lie in the video game market and virtual reality, where I aim to push boundaries and craft fun, immersive experiences.

Demonstratimg and promoting various board and card games for Asmodee. I am part of the demonstration team for Asmodee. I have worked at the UK Games Expo held at The NEC in Birmingham.
