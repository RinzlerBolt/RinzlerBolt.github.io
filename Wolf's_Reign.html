Project Details

Welcome to the adrenaline-filled world of “Wolf’s Reign,” a 2D Action-Adventure, Hack-n-Slash, set in a constantly revolving sci-fi world, blending intense combat mechanics, with a gripping narrative. You take the role of a formidable warrior with unparalleled combat skills, navigating dystopian environments, where chaos and the fabric of time collide. Drawing inspiration from iconic titles like Devil May Cry and Metal Gear Rising: Revengeance, the game promises a relentless journey through the immersive universe. This game felt like a challenge where I had to refine the combat system to having failsafes inside. it genuinely felt good once I had the Devil May Cry juggle style fully working.

Post-Mortem Reflection

Wolf's Reign is a game I’m continuing to work on in my spare time. I believe with further refinement, it will truly shine. After upgrading the project to Unity 6, a few smaller issues emerged, particularly with the movement system, as Velocity has transitioned to Linear Velocity. I’m currently addressing these issues, but so far, nothing too critical has been affected.
