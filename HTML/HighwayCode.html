Post-Mortem Reflection

This game is one that I take great pride in, as it truly tested my collaboration, teamwork, and leadership skills. Despite numerous changes during development, we remained focused on our end goal: delivering a fun yet challenging experience. Initially, the concept revolved around players adhering to the highway code, with penalties for breaking laws that triggered a variety of world states to disrupt progress. However, we shifted to a system of dynamic, ever-changing world states, with only one active at a time to avoid overwhelming the player. This project holds a special place for me, as it fueled my passion for the game industry and reinforced my dedication to crafting engaging experiences.
